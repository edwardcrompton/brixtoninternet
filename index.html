Brixton Internet

We write code for stuff that matters.

Brixton Internet was started with the belief that web technology can help people be organised, informed and heard.

We'll build you tools to help you do the amazing things you're doing, better.

Our Values

  • Fairness

    We support projects that promote fairness and equality and support those of us who are most vulnerable. This is reflected in our relationships with our customers and the types of projects we like to work on.

  • Transparency

    We strive to be open and honest. We will not try to hid problems or bugs in our software or our processes because we believe that by allowing other people to scrutinise our code we can build something better.

  • Sustainability

    We strive continually to become more environmentally sustainable. We believe we must all take responsibility for what we consume and we must always look for better alternatives. We like to work on projects that share these aims.

  • Appropriate technology

    We will not recommend our software services over others if we know there's more appropriate technology for your needs, as long as that software is aligned with our wider values.

  • Systems that work

    We strive to provide software that will help you achieve your aims and together we will refine systems we build to be as effective as they can be.
